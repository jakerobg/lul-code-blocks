Zero in on a site with Zoning Slices

Zoning Slices are the only product on the market that computes prevailing zoning regulations: every overlapping base and overlay district flattened into non-overlapping polygons, each with exactly one set of governing rules. Slices allow you to screen parcels in your market by density, use, parking requirements, and more—then rank sites by as-of-right potential.

See district rules with Zoning Districts

Zoning Districts offer GIS layers with permitted uses and dimensional requirements for every base and overlay district. Choose your geographies and regulatory characteristics for cross-market comparison. Whereas Slices compute prevailing regulations, Districts offer everything on a district-by-district basis.

Site industrial and commercial uses with Zoning Rasters

Zoning Rasters distill permitted residential and nonresidential uses into 30x30m grid cells spanning more than 1 billion acres, giving you a continuous surface of where nonresidential development is allowed rather than a patchwork of separate codes. Screen entire regions in one pass, spot suitable corridors, and feed the grid straight into your own models.

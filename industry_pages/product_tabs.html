Can I add an accessory dwelling unit (ADU)?

Sometimes called casitas or granny flats, ADUs let seniors downsize while staying close to family, or allow kids to move back home and still be independent. ADUs can also bring in income toward the mortgage or retirement. Your Parcel Report shows whether you can build one and who can occupy it, before you call an architect.

Can I rent out my ADU?
